Burger

Descritpion

Burgers are a classic favorite, perfect for a quick and satisfying meal. Start by shaping ground beef into patties and seasoning them with salt and pepper. Cook the patties on a grill or stovetop until they reach your desired level of doneness. While the patties cook, toast the burger buns and prepare your favorite toppings, like lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles, and cheese.

Assemble your burger by placing the patty on the bottom bun, adding your toppings, and finishing with the top bun. For extra flavor, spread some mayonnaise, ketchup, or mustard on the buns. Serve with a side of fries or a fresh salad for a complete meal. Burgers are customizable, so feel free to get creative with your ingredients!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb (450g) ground beef
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 burger buns
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Sliced tomatoes
  • Sliced onions
  • Pickles
  • Cheese slices (e.g., cheddar or American)
  • Condiments (e.g., ketchup, mustard, mayonnaise)

Steps

  1. Shape Patties: Divide ground beef into 4 equal portions and shape into patties. Season with salt and pepper.

  3. Toast Buns: Lightly toast the burger buns.
  4. Assemble: Place patty on the bottom bun, add toppings and condiments, and finish with the top bun.
  5. Serve: Enjoy with fries or a side salad!
