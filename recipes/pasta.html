Pasta

Descritpion

Pasta is a versatile and comforting dish that’s quick to prepare. Start by boiling your favorite pasta (like spaghetti or penne) in salted water until al dente. While the pasta cooks, prepare a simple sauce. A classic option is marinara: sauté garlic in olive oil, add canned tomatoes, and simmer with herbs like basil and oregano. Toss the cooked pasta in the sauce, and you’re ready to serve!

For a creamy twist, try Alfredo sauce: melt butter, add heavy cream and grated Parmesan cheese, and stir until smooth. Mix with fettuccine for a rich and indulgent meal. Top with fresh parsley or extra Parmesan for added flavor. Pasta is perfect for a quick weeknight dinner or a cozy weekend meal!

Ingredients

  • 8 oz (225g) pasta (e.g., spaghetti, penne, or fettuccine)
  • 1 tablespoon salt (for boiling water)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14 oz/400g) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 cup (240ml) heavy cream
  • 1 cup (100g) grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Cook Pasta: Boil pasta in salted water until al dente, then drain.
  2. For marinara: Sauté garlic in olive oil, add tomatoes and herbs, and simmer.
  3. For Alfredo: Melt butter, add cream and Parmesan, and stir until smooth.
  4. Combine: Toss pasta in the sauce.
  5. Serve: Top with fresh herbs or extra cheese.
