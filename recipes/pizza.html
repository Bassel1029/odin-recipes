Pizza

Descritpion

Making pizza at home is a fun and rewarding experience! Start by preparing the dough: mix flour, yeast, water, salt, and a bit of olive oil, then knead until smooth. Let the dough rise for about an hour until it doubles in size. While the dough rises, prepare your toppings. Classic options include tomato sauce, mozzarella cheese, pepperoni, mushrooms, and fresh basil. Feel free to get creative with your favorite ingredients!

Once the dough is ready, roll it out into a circle or rectangle, depending on your preference. Spread a layer of tomato sauce over the dough, then sprinkle generously with cheese and add your toppings. Bake in a preheated oven at 475°F (245°C) for 10–15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly. Slice, serve, and enjoy your delicious homemade pizza!

Ingredients

  • 2 ½ cups (300g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 packet (7g) active dry yeast
  • ¾ cup (180ml) warm water
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • ½ cup (120ml) tomato sauce (or pizza sauce)
  • 1 ½ cups (150g) shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Pepperoni slices (or your favorite toppings)
  • Fresh basil leaves (optional)
  • Olive oil (for drizzling)

Steps

  1. Make the Dough: Mix flour, yeast, salt, sugar, warm water, and olive oil. Knead until smooth, then let rise for 1 hour.
  2. Preheat Oven: Heat to 475°F (245°C).
  3. Roll Dough: Roll out the dough into a circle or rectangle.
  4. Add Toppings: Spread tomato sauce, sprinkle cheese, and add toppings.
  5. Bake: Bake for 10–15 minutes until crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.
  6. Serve: Slice and enjoy!
